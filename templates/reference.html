Introduction

The purpose of this website is to help migrant workers confirm whether they are treated fairly at work and to help them find effective ways to safeguard labour rights.


Overview of Data Sources

The data on this site is taken from the following official websites.

  • Australian Bureau of Statistics, Earnings and working conditions | Australian Bureau of Statistics (abs.gov.au)
  • Get ready for work - Fair Work
  • Fair Work Information Statement
  • Maximum weekly hours and the National Employment Standards (fairwork.gov.au)
  • Interactive safety hazard diagram downloads and illustrations (accessible) | SafeWork NSW
  • Pay guides - Fair Work
